In Italian, when expressing someone's age, '[['Avere' is 'to have' in Italian|avere]] [['Anno' is 'year' in Italian|anni]]' rather than '[['Essere' is 'to be' in Italian|essere]] anni' is used. 

For example, the phrase '{avere} {[[List of numbers in Italian|numero]]} anni' is used to say someone's age: 'Pietro *ha dieci anni*'
