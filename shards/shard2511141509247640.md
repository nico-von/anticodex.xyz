[['lo' is 'the' in Italian|'lo']] is used as the article 'the' in Italian for singular masculine words (like [['Il' is Italian for 'the' (for singular masculine words)|'il']]) but with the condition that the word must begin with 'z-' (or [['lo' is used as the article 'the' in Italian for singular masculine words that begins with 's-'|'s-']])
