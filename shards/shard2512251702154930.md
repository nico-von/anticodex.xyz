List of Italian prepositions
1. [['di' is the Italian for 'of' or 'from']]
2. [['a' is the locational, directional, 'to' in Italian]]
3. [['da' is 'from' in Italian]]
4. [['in' is also 'in' in Italian]]
5. [['Con' means 'with' in Italian]]
6. [['Su' is 'on' in Italian]]
7. [['per' is 'for' in Italian]]
8. [['fra' is 'between' in Italian]]
