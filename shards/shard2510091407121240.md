The conjugations of 'to be'([['Essere' is 'to be' in Italian|'essere']]) in Italian
1. [['Io sono', or 'sono', is 'I am' in Italian]]
2. [['Sei' is 'you are' in Italian]]
3. [['Siamo' is 'we are' in Italian]]
4. [['Sono' is 'they are' in Italian]]
5. [['siete' is 'you are'(plural) in Italian]]
6. [[An open 'e' sound, spelled 'è', in Italian means 'third person..is']] it is, she is, he is

Past tense
1. [['ero' is the past tense of 'sono'(I am) in Italian]]
2. [['era' is the past tense of 'è' in Italian]]
3. [['erano' is the past tense of 'sono' in Italian]]
4. [['eravamo' is the past tense of 'siamo' in Italian]]

Future tense
1. [['sarò' is the future tense of '(io)sono' in Italian]]
2. [['sarà' is the future tense of 'è' in Italian]]
3. [['saranno' is the future tense of '(loro)sono' in Italian]]
