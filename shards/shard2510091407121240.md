The conjugations of 'to be' in Italian
1. [['Io sono', or 'sono', is 'I am' in Italian]]
2. [['Sei' is 'you are' in Italian]]
3. [['Siamo' is 'we are' in Italian]]
4. [['Sono' is 'they are' in Italian]]
