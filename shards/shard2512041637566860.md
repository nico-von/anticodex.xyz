'fa' is the inverse of [['fra' is 'within'(as in something will occur in a certain timeframe) in Italian|'fra' (meaning within a certain timeframe)]]

'Fa' means 'ago' in Italian

Do not confuse it with the verb [['fa' is 'he, she, or it does' in Italian|'fa']]
