1. [[That the first rule of strategy is to anticipate the future moves of other players and then work backwards from there]] 
	1. Interaction is [[A 'sequential interaction' of decisions means that the decisions of players occur in turns, like Chess|sequential]]
	2. Tool is [[Backward reasoning in game theory|Backward reasoning]] often with [[About decision trees|decision trees]]
2. [[That the second rule of strategy is to always use the 'dominant strategy' if available]] 
	1. Interaction is [[A 'simultaneous interaction' of decisions simply means that the decisions of players are made at the same time, and each player is aware of the existence of other players|simultaneous]]
	2. Tool is [[A tool for visually analysing a 'simultaneous game' is by utilising a 'payoff table' where all the 'payoffs' for each of the combination of choices are laid out|payoff table]]
