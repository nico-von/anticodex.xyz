In the way as [[In Italian, 'si' is used to refer to oneself(in the third person) when a subject does the action on themselves|'si' is used to refer to oneself(the third person) when a subject does an action on themselves]], the following pronouns should be similar to it in a way that the subject acts upon themselves in Italian

1. [['Mi' is 'myself' in Italian]]
2. [['you' in Italian is 'ti'|'ti' as in 'yourself']]
3. 'si' (oneself)
4. [['ci' is 'us', 'each other', 'one another', 'ourselves' in Italian|'ci' as 'ourself']]
