Expressing 'millione'('million') in Italian is similar to the way it is said in English

1 million = un millione
2 million = [['due' is 2 in Italian|due]] [[Most Italian words that end in '-o' or '-e' get their plural form by transforming these endings to '-i'|millioni]]
