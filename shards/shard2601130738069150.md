Formal
1. [['Lei' is 'you'(formal) in Italian]]
2. [['{article} qual... -e, -i' is  'who' or 'that', like 'che' in Italian but is used when you want the gender stated to prevent ambiguity]]
3. [['essi' is the pronoun 'they' in Italian]]
4. [['esse' is a synonym of 'loro' in Italian]]
5. [['egli' is another word for the pronoun 'he' in Italian]]
6. [['essa' is another singular third person feminine pronoun in Italian]]

Informal
1. [['ciao' can be used when greeting friends in Italian]]
