'Suo' or 'Sua' is the possessive 'his' or 'her' in Italian

Remember, that [[Unlike English, where 'his' is used if the possessor is 'male' and 'her' if 'female', in Italian, the gender of the possessive word depends on the gender of the thing possessed, not of the possessor]]
