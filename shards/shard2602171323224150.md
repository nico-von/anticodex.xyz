That in [[The 'Tit for tat' strategy is to cooperate first and then to copy what others have done to you in the previous period|'tit for tat']], an infinite loop of defections is possible as it does not let defections happen unpunished. This could happen for instance if the opponent always defects, or if the opponent practises tit for tat too.
