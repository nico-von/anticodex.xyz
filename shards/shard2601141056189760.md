[[In Italian, 'si' is used to refer to oneself(in the third person) when a subject does the action on themselves|'si']] can also be used to say 'one' like how 'si dice' means 'one says' in Italian

[['si' used as an impersonal pronoun to mean 'one' can be used with 'ci' to imply (a previously understood context) as in 'ci si' in Italian]]
