A player's choices in a game are not motivated by rewards alone, but also by concerns for fairness, equality, altruism or even a desire of exacting vengeance or of repaying favours.

These are [[When thinking strategically, the perspective of other players must always be considered - their information, their motivation(motives), what they think of you, whether they're irrational or not etc. Because you are playing against people, not machines|not to be ignored]].
