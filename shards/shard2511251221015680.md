In Italian, some words that begin with a vowel can be [[In Italian, some words that are used in succession with certain words can be contracted together to form a single word|attached]] to the end of numbers by dropping the ending vowel of the number, sometimes replacing it with an apostrophe

Examples
1. [['trentun' is 31 in Italian]]
2. [['ventiquattr'ore' is 'twenty-four hours' in Italian]]
