Italian words relating toelating to positions:
1. [['Qui' is 'here' in Italian]]
2. [['la' is 'there' in Italian]], also, [['ci' is 'there' in Italian]]
3. [['fra' is 'between' in Italian]]
4. [['Quell...-a,-e,-o,-i' is 'that one'(the 'spatial' 'that', i.e. for pointing something) in Italian]]
5. [['da' is 'from' in Italian]] and [['a' is the locational, directional, 'to' in Italian]]
