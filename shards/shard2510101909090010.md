Italian words relating to positions:
1. [['Qui' is 'here' in Italian]]
2. [['là' is 'there' in Italian]] and [['Lì' is 'there'(pointing) in Italian]]
3. [['fra' is 'between' in Italian]]
4. [['Quel...-la,-le,-lo,-li' is 'that one'(the 'spatial' 'that', i.e. for pointing something) in Italian]]
5. [['da' is 'from' in Italian]] and [['a' is the locational, directional, 'to' in Italian]]
6. [['insieme' is 'together' in Italian]]
7. [['davanti a' is 'in front of' in Italian]] and [['dietro a' is 'behind of' in Italian]]
8. [['Su' is 'on'(above, up) in Italian]]
9. [['sotto, sopra' is 'under, above' in Italian]]
10. [['in' is also 'in' in Italian]], also, [['ci' is 'in it' in Italian]]
11. [['destra, sinistra' are 'right, left' in Italian]] 
12.  [['Questa' is 'this' in Italian]]
13. [['verso' is 'towards' in Italian]]
14. [['accanto' is 'next to' in Italian]]
