As [['non è...né' is used to say 'is neither...nor' in Italian]], 'né' can be used to enumerate what something is 'not' in a series

For example: 'It is neither an 'A', nor a 'B', nor a 'C'' - > 'Non è una 'A' né una 'B' né una 'C''
