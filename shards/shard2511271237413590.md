Italian words for sequences
1. [['Primo' is 'first' in Italian]] and [['ultimo' is 'last' in Italian]]
2. [['Prima di' is before and 'dopo' is 'after' in Italian]]
3. [['principio', 'fine' are 'beginning' and 'ending' in Italian]]
4. [['durano' is 'they last' in Italian]], [['dura' is 'it, he, or she lasts' in Italian]] and [['fino' is 'until' in Italian]]
5. [['Volta' is 'time' in the sense of occurence in Italian]]
6. [['di nuovo' is 'again'(do something once more)in Italian]]
