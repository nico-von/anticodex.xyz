The different [[Verb conjugations to indicate the doer of the action in Italian|conjugations]] of the [[The Italian word 'Fare' is irregular|irregular verb 'fare']]
1. [['Faccio' is 'I make or do' in Italian]]
2. [['Tu fai' is 'you make' or 'you do' in Italian]]
3. [['fa' is 'he, she, or it does' in Italian]]
4. [['facciamo' is 'we do or we make' in Italian]]
