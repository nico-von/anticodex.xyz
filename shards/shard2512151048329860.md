The root for the verb [['lava' is 'he, she or it washes' in Italian|'lava']] is a [[Verb conjugations to indicate the doer of the action in Italian|regular verb]] in Italian

And it is 'lavare' because [[In Italian, the regular '-are' verb form when conjugated to show that the action is done by a third person, i.e. 'he', 'she', or, 'it', its 'to {verb}' ending is replaced with '-a']]

Therefore, the conjugations of 'lavare' are thus:
1. 'lavo'
2. 'lavi'
3. 'lava'
4. 'laviamo'
5. 'lavate'
6. 'lavano'
