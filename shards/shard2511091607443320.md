In Italian, numbers in the ones place are simply attached(sometimes with some alteration e.g. [['trentun' is 31 in Italian|'trentun' = 31]] has not the 'a' from [['trenta' is 30 in Italian|'trenta' = 30]]) to those in the tens place, like English, as in 'forty-five' becomes 'quarantacinque' from [['quaranta' is 40 in Italian|'quaranta']] and [['cinque' is 5 in Italian|'cinque']].
