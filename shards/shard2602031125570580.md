'Non è [['nulla' is 'nothing' in Italian|nulla]]' is 'it's nothing' in Italian

Indeed, translating it literally as 'it is not nothing' would not make sense in English, instead it is to be understood as [['niente' is 'anything' if the sentence is negative in Italian|'it isn't anything']]
