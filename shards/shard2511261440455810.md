'[['un', 'uno', 'una' is 1 in Italian|un]] [['Quarto' is 'fourth' in Italian|quarto]]' is literally 'one-fourth' in Italian

Usually used to say 'a quarter'

Example: 15 minutes = 1/4 hour = un quarto d'[['Ora' is 'hour' in Italian|ora]]
