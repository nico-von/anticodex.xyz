What [[The 'Tit for tat' strategy is to cooperate first and then to copy what others have done to you in the previous period|'tit for tat']] offers:
1. It is clear and simple, not hard to calculate
2. It does not, by itself, start cheating
3. It punishes defections
4. It forgives defections
