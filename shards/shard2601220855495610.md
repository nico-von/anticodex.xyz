1. [['I love you' in Italian is 'ti voglio bene' which literally means 'You I want well']]
2. [['non fa niente' is 'it doesn't matter' in Italian]]
3. [['senza {to verb} niente' is 'without {to verb} anything' in Italian]]
4. [['è sceso giù' is '(he,she, or it) went down' in Italian]]
5. [['da {who owns the place}' is 'at (who owns the place) place' in Italian]]
6. [['Non è nulla' is 'it's nothing' in Italian]]
7. [['un paio di scarpe' is 'a pair of shoes' in Italian]], [['un paio di settimane' is 'a couple of weeks' in Italian]]
8. [['intanto che...' is 'while' in Italian]]
9. [['in cui', 'a cui' means 'in which','to which' in Italian]]
