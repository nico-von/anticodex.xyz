The Italian word [['Quando' is 'When' in Italian|'Quando']] can sometimes be used with 'è che' to indicate 'When is it that…'. [[An open 'e' sound, spelled 'è', in Italian means 'third person..is'|'è' means is]] and [['che' is both 'what' and 'that' in Italian|'che' means that]].
