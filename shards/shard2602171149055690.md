The 'Tit for tat' strategy is to cooperate first and then to copy what others have done to you in the previous period

[['Tit for tat' has been tried against many other strategies(as computer programs) in the early 1980s and was proved to be the best strategy for playing the prisoner's dillema]]

[[What 'tit for tat' offers - 'clarity', 'niceness', 'provocability', 'forgivingness']]

[[The disadvantages of 'tit for tat'-that it only results to losses and ties, that it is possible for an infinite loop of defections to happen]]

[[The advantages of 'tit for tat' - that it never gets left behind, that it encourages cooperation]]
