'zii', 'zie', is the plural of [['zio', 'zia', is 'uncle', 'aunt' in Italian|'zio', 'zia']] in Italian
### Excerpt
![[jensenLItalianoSecondoMetodo1962excerptindex#^m8hru]]

Unlike [['Mio' is made plural as 'miei' instead of 'mii', so as to separate the two consecutive 'i's that would otherwise be awkward to pronounce|'mio' becoming 'miei']],'zii' is formed with two consecutive 'i's instead of the two 'i's being separated with an 'e'
