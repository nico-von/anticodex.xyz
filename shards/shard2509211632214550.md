It is important to distinguish between the 'e' sound of the Italian words [[An open 'e' sound, spelled 'è', in Italian means 'third person..is'|'è']] and [[A weaker and closed 'e' sound in Italian means 'and'|'e']]. This is because [[In Italian, words must be clearly pronounced as they may lead to misunderstanding|it changes the meaning of the word, leading to misunderstanding]], unlike [[The 'e' sound of the Italian word 'è' when distinguishing it with that of 'che' varies differently between dialects.|the 'e' sound when distinguishing between'è' and 'che']].
