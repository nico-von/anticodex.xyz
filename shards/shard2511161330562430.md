When the noun it follows begin with [['s' and 'z' are irregular consonants in Italian|'irregular consonants']] or vowles:

1. [[In Italian, 'Gli' is the article 'the' for plural words that are masculine and start with vowels]], [[In Italian, 'Gli' is the article 'the' for plural words that are masculine and start with 'z-'|'z-']]
