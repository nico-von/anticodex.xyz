The decisions of players in a game of strategy depend on each other, either [[A 'sequential interaction' of decisions means that the decisions of players occur in turns, like Chess|sequentially]] or [[A 'simultaneous interaction' of decisions simply means that the decisions of players are made at the same time, and each player is aware of the existence of other players|simultaneously]]
