The different [[Verb conjugations to indicate the doer of the action in Italian|conjugations]] of the [[The Italian word 'Dire' is an irregular verb|irregular verb 'dire']]
1. [['Dico' is 'I say' in Italian]]
2. [['dici' is 'you say' in Italian]]
3. [['dice' is 'he, she, it says' in Italian]]
4. [['diciamo' is 'we say' in Italian]]
5. [['dicono' is 'they say' in Italian]]
