[['lo' is 'the' in Italian|'lo']] is used as the article 'the' in Italian for singular masculine words(like [['Il' is Italian for 'the' (for singular masculine words)|'il']]) but with the condition that the word must begin with 's-' (or [['lo' is used as the article 'the' in Italian for singular masculine words that begins with 'z-'|'z-']])(see [['s' and 'z' are irregular consonants in Italian]])
